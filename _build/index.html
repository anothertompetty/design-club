Where the creative community figure out what's next.

Every other month Design Club brings together designers of all disciplines and experience for drinks, good times, and original talks from leaders in our field. Speakers are urged to share candidly about their work, careers, and distinct perspectives on the industry. Events are free, and held in great locations around East London.

The speakers
Tom Blomfield — @t_blom
CEO and Founder, Mondo

Tom is CEO and co-founder of Mondo - a new smartphone-based bank. Mondo broke records in March 2016, raising £1m of crowdfunding in 96 seconds. Tom previously founded GoCardless, a fintech startup that has gone on to raise $25m. He’ll be sharing the stage with Hugo to talk about building a design-led business.
Hugo Cornejo — @hugocornejo
Head of Design, Mondo

Hugo has been part of Mondo since the beginning, defining the design principles that shape an easy to use, yet powerful, bank and ‘home screen app’. Back in Spain he helped big corporations (BBVA, Globalia) and startups (Ticketea, Chicisimo) to design world-class customer experiences.
Alexis Cuddyre — @omgimmarried
Head of Creative and Brand, ADAY

Previously Creative Director at Brand Union and co-founder of Bespoky, Alexis runs Brand and Creative at technical apparel startup, ADAY. Having recently moved to New York, she’ll be sharing her experiences of scaling a contemporary retail business in a tough market and what it means to create a new genre of clothing.
